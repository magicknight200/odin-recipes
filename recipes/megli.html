megli :

Description :

megli is one of the most popular dishes in tunisia , it is easy to make , it doesn't have much ingrediants and it takes 10-15 minutes

ingrediants :

  • eggs
  • potatoes
  • cooking oil
  • tomatos
  • pepper

steps :

  1. cut out the potatoes in the desired shape u want to cook
  2. put a little cooking oil in the pan and let it heat up a little bit
  3. put the potatos in the pan and let them heat up untill theyre cooked
  4. take out your plate and put your cooked potates in it
  5. next,let the tomatos and pepper in the pan till theyre cooked
  6. add them to your dish
  7. now take your eggs and cook them and add them to your plate
  8. boom you have a megli enjoy your meal
