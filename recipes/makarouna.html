makarouna :

Description :

makarouna is one of the best and most delicious tunisian dishes , it is not so hard to make and doesnt take much time

there are 2 types of makarouna , the spicy one and the sweet one , they're almost the same its just the spicy one needs some kind of sauce that you must cook and i dont know how to make it so i will teach you how to cook the sweet makarouna

once you know how to cook the sweet makarouna the procedure is all the same you just add the sauce in the end instead of the sugar and butter .

ingrediants

  • makarouna(it is selled in all the stores in tunisia)
  • water
  • sugar
  • butter

Steps

  1. bring a casserole and fill it with water
  2. put the casserole and fire and wait for the water to boil
  3. once the water is boiled put the makarouna in it and keep moving it for 5-10 mins
  4. use a straining casserole or any other tool to seperate the water from the makarouna
  5. add sugar and butter to your hot makarouna and mix them well untill both butter and sugar are melted in the makarouna
  6. enjoy your meal
