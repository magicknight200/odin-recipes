Coffe

Description :

meanwhile coffee is not a dish it is one of my favourite drinks , it is sweet and it gives me energy and it keeps my day positive and productive

another advantage of coffe is that you can drink it anywhere , on an empty stomach or a full stomach and it has very few ingrediants and it takes 10-15 minutes to make.

ingredients

  • milk
  • nescafee
  • sugar

steps

  1. fill your cup with the amount of milk you want to drink
  2. put the milk in the milk pan and heat it untill its warm
  3. back to the cup , put 2-3 spoons of sugar and 1-2 spoons of nescaffe
  4. after the milk is warm , pour a little bit of it in the cup and mix it with the nescaffee and sugar really hard
  5. pour the rest of the milk and mix the shake
  6. you got yourself a warm cappucino drink it slowely and enjoy it .
